Agario

Description

A simple little cell game. When cells overlap, the bigger cell will eat the smaller cell. The green cell is you and will follow your cursor. The small circles are food. Lighter food will grow cells that eat it. Darker food is poisonous and will shrink cells that eat it. Enemies split when they grow too big and you are limited in size, making this a game about how long you can survive rather than how big you can get.
